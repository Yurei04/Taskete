CPR Course Content

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) is a life-saving technique used in emergencies such as cardiac arrest. Key steps include:

  • Check for responsiveness and breathing.
  • Call for emergency help.
  • Start chest compressions at a rate of 100-120 per minute.
  • Provide rescue breaths after every 30 compressions if trained.

First Aid Course Content

First Aid is the immediate care given to someone who is injured or ill. Topics include:

  • Managing bleeding and wounds.
  • Using the recovery position for unconscious individuals.
  • Handling burns and scalds.
  • Recognizing and managing shock.

Fire Safety Course Content

Fire safety involves understanding and preventing fire hazards. Key points:

  • Identify common fire risks in homes and workplaces.
  • Know how to use a fire extinguisher (PASS method).
  • Understand evacuation procedures.
  • Learn how to prevent grease fires and electrical fires.
